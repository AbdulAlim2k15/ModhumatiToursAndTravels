About Us

Madhumati Tours and Travels is a forward-thinking travel agency based in Bangladesh, committed to delivering exceptional travel experiences for all. With a legacy rooted in business excellence, we began our journey in the 1990s as Madhumati Tiles Ltd. Over the years, we’ve expanded into diverse sectors, including cold storage, ice mills, and drinking water production.

Founded in 2024 by Farzana Sabur, who brings over 20 years of industry expertise, Madhumati Tours and Travels is dedicated to offering comprehensive, 360-degree travel solutions. Our experienced team combines modern technology with personalized service to meet the evolving needs of today’s travelers.
